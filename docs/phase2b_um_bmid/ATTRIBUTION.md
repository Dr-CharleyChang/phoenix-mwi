# P2-B UM-BMID and ORR attribution

The files in this directory were generated by Phoenix-MWI from selected measurements and metadata in the University of Manitoba Breast Microwave Imaging Dataset (UM-BMID), Gen-One.

Dataset publication: T. Reimer, J. Krenkevich, and S. Pistorius, “An open-access experimental dataset for breast microwave imaging,” EuCAP 2020, DOI [10.23919/EuCAP48036.2020.9135659](https://doi.org/10.23919/EuCAP48036.2020.9135659).

Data record: [UM-BMID on Zenodo, DOI 10.5281/zenodo.5120981](https://zenodo.org/records/5120981), licensed CC-BY-4.0.

Official dataset software/documentation: [UManitoba-BMS/UM-BMID](https://github.com/UManitoba-BMS/UM-BMID), licensed Apache-2.0.

ORR publication: T. Reimer and S. Pistorius, “An Optimization-Based Approach to Radar Image Reconstruction in Breast Microwave Sensing,” Sensors 21(24), 8172 (2021), DOI [10.3390/s21248172](https://doi.org/10.3390/s21248172), CC-BY-4.0.

Official ORR software: [TysonReimer/ORR-Algorithm](https://github.com/TysonReimer/ORR-Algorithm), licensed Apache-2.0.

Phoenix independently implements the published primary-scatter forward/adjoint and optimization concept with bounded iterations, exact adjoint tests, numerical scaling, optional regularization, and backtracking. It does not copy the public repository source files and does not claim to reproduce the paper's Gen-Three diagnostic study; this benchmark uses Gen-One.

Phoenix-MWI does not redistribute the official archive or extracted pickle files. The generated JSON, Markdown, and figures retain this attribution and make no clinical claim.
